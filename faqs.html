Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Are your ingredients organic?

  A. All of our ingredients are organic and come from local farmers who farm sustainably.


Q. Do you use juice concentrates or is everything freshly squeezed?

  A. All of the fruits used in our juice are freshly squeezed every single day.


Q. Do you use artifical ingredients, flavors, or colors in your juices?

  A. All of our ingredients are natural. We do not use any artificial ingredients, flavors, or colors.


Q. How many locations do you have, and where are they?

  A. We have three locations, and they are all located in Florida. You can see the complete list of all of our addresses on the locations page.


Q. Is sugar added to your drinks? If so, how much?

  A. We do not add any sugar to our drinks.
