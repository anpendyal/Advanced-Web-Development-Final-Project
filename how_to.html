The Jumpin' Juice Company is committed to serving people fresh, healthy, and tasty drinks. Here, we'll give you a recipe so that you can create your very own healthy, delicious drink!

Cranberry Juice Tea

This recipe is very simple to make and easy to serve. You can prepare this drink in under 10 minutes. Enjoy this fresh drink on a warm day.

Ingredients
  • 4 regular sized tea bags
  • 4 cups of boiling water
  • 12-ounce can of frozen rasperry-cranberry juice concentrate
  • 4 cups of cold water
How to Make Cranberry Juice Tea
  1. Place tea bags in a large measuring cup. Pour the boiling hot water over. Let steep for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove tea bags and chill tea in the refrigerator. At serving time, combine juice and cold water in a large pitcher. Pour in the refrigerated tea.
  3. Stir well and serve with ice.
