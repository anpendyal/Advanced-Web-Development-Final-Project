About Us

Welcome to "The Jumpin' Juice Company!" Here, we serve fresh, healthy juices at affordable prices. All of our ingredients are natural and organic and contain no added sugar. Our juices are created from fresh fruits that we squeeze everyday.

The Jumpin' Juice Company was founded in 2011 by a pair of sisters that wanted to share their healthy and tasty juice recipies with others.

The Jumpin' Juice Company supports local farmers and sustainable farming. We buy our produce from local farmers that farm sustainably. Sustainable farming is farming in a way that future generations can also use the land. For example, they do not use pesticides. Our farmers also use organic fertilizers.
